# Security Audit – Botium Toys

## Overview
A complete internal security audit of Botium Toys, a fictional toy company,
conducted as part of the Google Cybersecurity Certificate program.

## Audit Scope
- Assessed all assets managed by the IT department
- Evaluated existing controls against best practices
- Checked compliance with PCI DSS, GDPR, and SOC standards

## Key Findings
- 8 out of 14 controls were NOT in place
- All 4 PCI DSS best practices were non-compliant
- GDPR had partial compliance (2 of 4 practices met)
- SOC had partial compliance (2 of 4 practices met)

## Top Risks Identified
1. No encryption on customer credit card data
2. No least privilege or separation of duties
3. No disaster recovery plan or backups
4. No intrusion detection system (IDS)
